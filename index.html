Memory!

Rules of the Game!

The rules are simple. Here they are as follows:

  • The game starts with a grid of 16 tiles turned face down.
  • There are 8 matching pairs randomly distributed in the grid.
  • You are allowed to turn two tiles at a time trying to find matching pairs.
  • If two tiles match, they remain turned over.
  • If two tiles don't match, both tiles are flipped back over.
  • The game ends when all pairs have been matched.
